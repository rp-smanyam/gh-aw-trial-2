# 2. Focus on Applicant Instead of Prospect Voice

## Status

Accepted (2025-07-11)

## Context

Renter AI Prospect Voice stabilized, and a decision was made to prepare it for an eventual release. The Applicant AI Team, despite its name, was working on implementing Renter AI Prospect Voice using a different technology stack, OpenAI's Agents SDK. This original decision was made because Renter AI Prospect Voice was having latency and "looping" issues. Those issues were mitigated, so it didn't make sense for the Applicant AI Team to also deliver Prospect Voice, especially since Renter AI behavior was intended to serve as a reference but was still in flux.

## Decision

The Applicant AI Team should focus on Applicant, its original purpose.

## Consequences

Applicant requirements are not ready as of July 20, 2025.
